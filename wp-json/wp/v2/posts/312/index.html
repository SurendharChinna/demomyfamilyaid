The classic sour candy, Sour Patch Kids had limited flavors in the past but now offers a ton of fun flavors and great gift options for candy connoisseurs of all ages. You can create the ultimate sweet and sour gift using the custom \u201cMake My Mix\u201d feature on the official Sour Patch Kids Website.\u00a0<\/p>\n

You start by choosing your favorite flavors including Redberry, Blue Raspberry, Pink Strawberry, Lemon, Orange, Lime, Grape, or White Pineapple. You can choose each flavor or just your favorites if you prefer having more of your favorites.<\/p>\n

Then you get to choose your packaging and preferred weight of candy ranging from 2-5 pounds with themes for celebrations including Christmas, birthdays, and other options for parties. There are also bags, tins, party favor treat bags (minimum order of 20), and party favor tins (minimum order of 10).<\/p>\n

The final part is personalizing your Sour Patch Kids mix with a name (limited to 12 characters) and you\u2019ll have your customized sweet treat delivered to you or the gift recipient. For true fans of Sour Patch Kids, the official website offers more than just candy.<\/p>\n

There is a holiday gift guide with fun gifts including an ugly holiday sweater, shirts, socks, candles, sweet beauty products, pillow, and plush toys. These are great options for gifts or stocking stuffers that the whole family can enjoy!\u00a0<\/p>\n
