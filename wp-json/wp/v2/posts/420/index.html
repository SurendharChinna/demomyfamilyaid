There are tons of movies and TV shows about haunted houses, especially with the TV series \u201cAmerican Horror Story\u201d that has some form of haunting one way or another. On-screen, there are clear signs of a haunting but in real life small signs can point to the reasoning that you might live in a haunted house.\u00a0<\/p>\n

Paranormal investigator Amanda Paulson gives insight on signs to determine if your house is haunted with ghosts that are typically energy or presence that manifests in different objects, areas, or forms in your home. Paulson explains, \u201cMy personal definition of a ghost is a shadow of life perceived in a way that defies reality. A ghost can be the intelligent spirit of the deceased, persisting after death because of unfinished business, or it can be residual energy of the living or deceased, imprinted onto a location or object.\u201d\u00a0<\/p>\n

Paulson reveals some indicators that can be telling while advising many hauntings a misunderstood which is why it\u2019s recommended to learn the history of your home that will help you identify possible paranormal activity. There are many means to learn about the history behind the property such as asking your real estate or leasing agent, searching public property records in your area, and even Google your home address to see if you can find any names of previous owners or tenants (pay attention to any obituaries.\u00a0<\/p>\n

Another option is learning more from neighbors that are familiar with the former owners or renters and possibly have some insight into the home\u2019s past. Paulson also recommends taking note of how you, house guests, and animals emotionally feel in the present taking inventory of feelings your home brings for any sense of unrest.\u00a0<\/p>\n
