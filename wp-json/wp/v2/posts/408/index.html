Disney World holds a special place in all our hearts. This October is the 50thanniversary of Disney World, and it is being celebrated in what is being known as \u201cThe World’s Most Magical Celebration”. Of course, we more than believe that the anniversary will be magical because what place is more magical than Disney World?\u00a0<\/p>\n

The 18-monthlong celebration starts this month, October 2021, and will continue until April2023! The celebration brings with it\u00a0the debut of new daytime and night time shows, new opening attractions at the different resorts, and many exciting and magical surprises to come over the next 18 months.\u00a0<\/p>\n

 <\/p>\n

Celebrate Across The Four Disney Parks<\/h3>\n

With Covid travel restrictions lifted across the country, this is the perfect opportunity to get your Disney World tickets and start preparing for a magical holiday. There are four amazing parks in different parts of the Walt Disney World Resort in Orlando, Florida, and they all have unique and amazing experiences to offer.\u00a0<\/p>\n

The Magical Kingdom Park, EPCOT, Disney\u2019s Animal Kingdom Theme Park, and Disney\u2019s Hollywood Studios are all set to have new anniversary events and attractions that you simply cannot miss.\u00a0<\/p>\n

 <\/p>\n

New Attractions At Disney World Florida<\/h3>\n

Besides all the usual magic and excitement of Disney World, the resort has introduced all new events and attractions, especially for the anniversary celebration. This includes new roller coasters at the EPCOT park and the Magic Kingdom park and a brand new\u00a0<\/span>Star Wars Galactic\u00a0<\/span><\/a>Star cruiser-themed hotel set to open in March 2022.<\/p>\n

There are going to be some epic firework extravaganzas that only Disney can do and some amazing new merchandise, so you can shop to your heart\u2019s content. The\u00a0<\/span>Walt Disney World Resort 50th Anniversary Collection<\/a>\u00a0has brand new clothes, hoodies, accessories, toys, pins, and so much more. There are also new collectibles for all Disney fans who love collecting merchandise.\u00a0<\/p>\n

Disney has also announced a brand new MagicBand+ technology that is going to enhance and remake your Disney experience. The MagicBand+ is a wearable band that wakes at different times and allows you to unlock different interactive sessions with the help of changing lights, gestures, and vibrations. This will change the way we experience Disney World’s immersive storytelling.\u00a0\u00a0<\/p>\n

<\/h3>\n

Ready For The Biggest Celebration?<\/h3>\n

The 50th anniversary of Disney World is set to be the biggest celebration in Disney\u2019s history. It will be celebrated with fireworks, luxury resort stays, amazing rides, great food, and the best souvenirs. We’re so excited to see what the next 18 months will bring and what new surprises and adventures await us.\u00a0<\/p>\n

If you, too, are a huge Disney fan, then now is the time to book your Disney World tickets, if you haven\u2019t already! We are sure that the anniversary celebration will be the best vacation of your life, and if you haven\u2019t been to Disney World before, then now is the perfect time to go and experience the magic of Disney.\u00a0<\/p>\n
