You might be familiar with buying your sauce in a jar but the traditional Italian sauce is homemade that is often a family recipe passed down generation after generation. With fresh tomatoes right from the garden, Italian mamas harvest all varieties of different kinds of tomatoes.\u00a0<\/p>\n

After they pick the tomatoes, they boil them all together in a pot and put a cover on the lid. They wait for five minutes to take them all out and then drain the water and remove all the left-over tomatoes. They then use a strainer machine to separate the tomatoes from the seeds. They then place it all in a terra-cotta bowl.\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0<\/p>\n

The true secret in making the perfect plate of Italian pasta is all in the flavor. This is a traditional Italian sauce bowl that is designed specifically to not alter the taste of the sauce. Then they salt and add a bit of sugar to remove the fatty acids. Now it’s time for the basil leaves and that’s it<\/p>\n
