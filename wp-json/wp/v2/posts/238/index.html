Living in the 21st century means being aware of your actions and encouraging sustainability in as many things as possible. While buying electric vehicles and saving water are some of the more-known attempts, you\u2019d be surprised by how you can adopt eco-friendly lifestyles with even the seemingly less-significant actions.<\/p>\n

One such example is when you\u2019re buying clothes, toys, food, and other necessities for your child. Baby product and manufacturing can involve critical procedures that can damage our environment.<\/p>\n

What better way to reduce the pollution and harmful effects than by shopping for environmentally friendly kids’ brands? Confused about what brands to buy from? We\u2019ll help you with yourmission. Below are nine of the safest and most eco-friendly businesses topurchase your child\u2019s necessities.<\/p>\n

\n

Beya Made<\/h3>\n

Beya Made promises of premium quality by using sustainable fabrics to design and stitch kids’ clothing. The brand allows you access to trendy and cutesy apparel, including overalls, shirts, pants, and rompers, all under one roof.<\/p>\n

\n

\n

Mini + Meep<\/h3>\n

The brand takes special care to keep its practices eco-friendly. Be labor sovereignty, planet protection, or ethics. Mini + Meep aces in all departments like a pro. It offers products for children and adults.<\/p>\n

 <\/p>\n

Ten Little<\/h3>\n

Looking for a sustainable brand to get your child\u2019s shoes from? Ten Little is the best store to head to. The company uses vegan leather uppers and 100% organic cotton lining in every footwear, making it free from PVC and phthalate. Even the insoles are made using recycled rubber.<\/p>\n

 <\/p>\n

Frugi<\/h3>\n

Frugi is certified by GOTS and Soil Association and specializes in manufacturing products made of recycled or organic materials. The brand offers clothes for infants, toddlers, and kids that feel soft on the skin and are free from toxins and chemicals.<\/p>\n

 <\/p>\n

Burt’s Bees Baby<\/h3>\n

Burt\u2019s Bees Baby enables you to shop for organic cotton-based kids’ clothing online and in person. The store offers outfits for newborns to 7-year-olds. Affordable, fashionable, adorable, and most importantly: environment-friendly; all these reasons make the business an excellent store to buy from.<\/p>\n

 <\/p>\n

Pact<\/h3>\n

Pact is famous for its comfy, cozy, and chic dressing, manufacturing shirts, pants, pajamas, dresses, and socks for children aged twelve months to twelve years. The price point is super pocket-friendly, and every item uses organic cotton with the labor working in safe working conditions.<\/p>\n

 <\/p>\n

Jackalo<\/h3>\n

The ecological business offers dresses, hair accessories, blouses, and pants for four years to fourteen years old kids. The family-owned brand values labor rights and promises zero disposal to encourage a greener environment.<\/p>\n

 <\/p>\n

The Good Tee<\/h3>\n

Be it the amiable working conditions for the employees or environment-friendly and ethical practices in producing snug cotton tee shirts. The Good Tee promises transparency in every stage.<\/p>\n

 <\/p>\n

Firebird Kids<\/h3>\n

Firebird Kids is the perfect spot to get gender-neutral kids’ clothing for your children aged two to thirteen. The zero-waste apparel is adorable, affordable, and durable to appease environmentalist parents.<\/p>\n
