As the world reopens after the COVID-19lockdown, it is only fair that you\u2019d want to take your family on vacation someplace far away. However, traveling to certain countries with children can be difficult even when we\u2019re not in the middle of a pandemic. Don\u2019t worry! We\u2019ve compiled a list of the top 10 places to travel to for families so that you don\u2019t have to spend hours researching the destination you wish to visit. You will still have to check in to know if all of these companies are open for tourism, but here are some of our picks!\u00a0<\/p>\n

<\/h3>\n

Singapore<\/h3>\n

It\u2019s a small country, but it\u2019s filled with activities for you and your kids to enjoy. You can visit Universal Studios or even enjoy some of the futuristic gardens. Singapore\u2019s national language is English. So, you shouldn\u2019t find traveling from one place to another difficult.\u00a0<\/p>\n

<\/h3>\n

Vietnam<\/h3>\n

Vietnam is a great country to visit with children. It has beaches and many historical sites. You can walk around towns and learn about the Vietnam War or enjoy nature at the Mekong Delta.\u00a0<\/p>\n

<\/h3>\n

Japan<\/h3>\n

It is one of the best countries to visit regardless of the size and age of your family. It is a clean and safe country with many exciting activities for you to take on with your family. You can go hiking or rafting and visit some of the most architecturally beautiful places in Asia.\u00a0<\/p>\n

<\/h3>\n

France<\/h3>\n

France is home to several museums and galleries that make it an excellent great historical site to visit as a child. Even if your child is not a big history buff, the Eiffel Tower at night is enough to hold anyone\u2019s gaze. Additionally, French food is to die for.\u00a0<\/p>\n

<\/h3>\n

Malaysia<\/h3>\n

It is a great gateway country to Southeast Asia if you\u2019ve never traveled here. It has beautiful clean beaches and tropical resorts where you and your family can witness nature at its finest. Malaysia is also home to many cultures. So, it\u2019s a great way to introduce your children to different cultures.\u00a0<\/p>\n

<\/h3>\n

Coast Rica<\/h3>\n

With its natural parks and beaches, Costa Rica is one of the most visited sites in Central America. You can take your kids ziplining or horseback riding even if they\u2019re young, so they can thoroughly enjoy themselves.\u00a0<\/p>\n

<\/h3>\n

Sri Lanka<\/h3>\n

It may seem like a small island but, Sri Lanka offers all the excitement of any larger tourist destination. It has protected areas for wildlife like leopards and elephants so your children can witness the wilderness.\u00a0<\/p>\n

<\/h3>\n

The United Kingdom<\/h3>\n

You can learn about the UK\u2019s history in museums and art galleries. The country is also home to Harry Potter and The Beatles. So, you can have a fun time with your kids while visiting tours and other experiences created around these pop culture giants.\u00a0<\/p>\n

<\/h3>\n

Thailand<\/h3>\n

Thailand is very child-friendly, and it has so much to do. You can go snorkeling in the ocean or visit temples and chat with Buddhist monks. Thailand has something for everyone.\u00a0<\/p>\n

<\/h3>\n

Australia<\/h3>\n

This massive island is one of the most diverse countries you or your kids will ever witness. It has ample wildlife, flora, and fauna. Most Australians speak English, so you don\u2019t have to worry about a language barrier here.\u00a0\u00a0\u00a0<\/p>\n

<\/h2>\n

We hope that this list of Top 10 places to travel for families simplified your choices for you. Ensure you know the quarantine rules and regulations for any country before you finalize your visit.\u00a0<\/p>\n
