Recipe<\/strong><\/span>
2 whole pre-made Trader Joe\u2019s gingerbread Molasses cookie dough as Pie Crust<\/span>
1 can of Organic Pumpkin (usually from Trader Joe\u2019s or Wholefoods)<\/span>
\u00bd Cup of Brown Sugar<\/span>
\u00bd\u00a0\u00a0Cup of butter<\/span>
\u00bd Cup white Sugar<\/span>
3 Large eggs<\/span>
1 Can of sweetened condensed milk<\/span>
1 Stick of melted butter<\/span>
1 Teaspoon of Vanilla Extract<\/span>
1 Teaspoon of cinnamon<\/span><\/p>\n

<\/span><\/p>\n

Directions<\/strong><\/span>
1. Pre-heat oven to 350F getting ready for the Trader Joe\u2019s Gingerbread Molasses Cookie Dough<\/span><\/p>\n

2. Then let bake for 15-20 minutes and let cool for 2 minutes after your dough is cooled and putting some baking powder on the counter.<\/span><\/p>\n

3. Combine the cookie dough into a ball then use a dough roller to flatten out the dough at a slime thick consistency.<\/span><\/p>\n

4. Use the inside of your backing pan also use the side to build up the dough crust with four extra stored Ginger Cookie Dough. To then refrigerate and cool.<\/span><\/p>\n

5. For the Pumpkin Pie Crack two eggs whisk thoroughly.<\/span><\/p>\n

6. Melt the butter in the microwave to a melt.<\/span><\/p>\n

7. Place the Flour, Vanilla Extract, Sugar, Cinnamon, Salt inside after the butter has cooled a little. Mix With a whisk.<\/span><\/p>\n

8. To Then put the canned Pumpkins in after mix with a mixer machine and place the sweetened condensed milk in and mix again with the mixer.<\/span><\/p>\n

9. Take out the cooled dough and then place the batter to be baked at 350F for 1 1\/2 hours.\u00a0<\/span><\/p>\n
