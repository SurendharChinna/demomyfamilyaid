SMS Privacy Policy<\/h1>\n

[\/et_pb_text][et_pb_text _builder_version=”4.22.2″ _module_preset=”default” global_colors_info=”{}”]<\/p>\n

We at My Family Aid understand that you value your privacy and we strive to respect that privacy when you are using our services. This Privacy Policy describes what information we gather from you, how we use that information and what we do to protect it. By using the My Family Aid website (www.myfamilyaid.com<\/a>), you are expressly consenting to the information handling practices described in this policy.<\/span><\/p>\n

<\/strong><\/p>\n

Emails and SMS Messages<\/strong><\/span><\/p>\n

Our FamAid services feature the distribution of content to your cellular phone, and by registering for a FamAid account, you agree to receive SMS messages to your phone, including messages regarding Services you request.<\/span><\/p>\n

<\/strong><\/p>\n

Technical Information<\/strong><\/span><\/p>\n

The My Family Aid website collects and retains technical information regarding visits to the My Family Aid website such as the IP address assigned to the computer visiting the site, the type of browser used by a visitor and the operating system running the user’s connections to the service. This data is used to analyze the ways in which our site is used, and how we can improve the user experience.<\/span><\/p>\n

<\/strong><\/p>\n

How We Use Your Information<\/strong><\/span><\/p>\n

My Family Aid uses your personal information in to provide you with our services, for administrative functions, to contact you and for internal research and reporting purposes.<\/span><\/p>\n

<\/strong><\/p>\n

Information Relating to Children<\/strong><\/span><\/p>\n

My Family Aid and the FamAid services are intended for users age 17 and up. By registering for a My Family Aid account, you represent that you are 17 years of age or older. If you are younger than 17, you agree that you received parental permission both to complete the registration process and to receive content to your cell phone. We will not knowingly collect personal information of children under 17 years of age, and if we learn that we have inadvertently collected such information, we will promptly delete it.<\/span><\/p>\n

<\/strong><\/p>\n

Disclosure of Personal Information<\/strong><\/span><\/p>\n

Disclosure of Personal Information<\/span>
We do not rent or share your personal information without permission or as described in this policy, including under the following circumstances.<\/span>
– when we believe in good faith that it is necessary to respond to subpoenas, court orders, or other legal process, or to exercise our legal rights or defend against legal claims;<\/span>
– when we believe in good faith it is necessary in order to investigate, prevent, or take action regarding illegal activities, suspected fraud, situations involving potential threats to the physical safety of any person, violations of our terms and conditions, or as otherwise required by law.<\/span>
Finally, we may transfer information, including any personally identifiable information, in the event My Family Aid is acquired by or merged with another company.<\/span><\/p>\n

<\/strong><\/p>\n

Protecting your information<\/strong><\/span><\/p>\n

My Family Aid takes reasonable measures to safeguard your personal information during transmission and when stored on our network. However, due to the open communication nature of the Internet, communications between you and the website, and the website and you, may not be secure, depending on the network you are using to communicate with us.<\/span>
If My Family Aid learns of a security breach through which sensitive personal information may have been compromised, we may notify you electronically so that you can take appropriate protective steps. By using the My Family Aid website (www.myfamilyaid.com<\/a>) or providing personal information to us through any means, you agree that we can communicate with you electronically regarding security, privacy, and administrative issues relating to your use of this site.<\/span>
If you have any questions about security on our website, you can contact us at\u00a0<\/span>help@myfamilyaid.com<\/span><\/a><\/span><\/p>\n

<\/strong><\/p>\n

Changes to this Privacy Policy<\/strong><\/span><\/p>\n

As we update and expand our Services, we reserve the right to modify this privacy policy at any time. Any changes to the policy will be posted at www.myfamilyaid.com\/sms-privacy-policy\/<\/a>. If we make material changes to this policy, we will notify you here, by email, or by means of a notice on our homepage. By continuing to use the My Family Aid (www.myfamilyaid.com<\/a>) website after changes to this policy, you agree to those changes.<\/span><\/p>\n

<\/strong><\/p>\n

Contact Information<\/strong><\/span><\/p>\n

If you have any questions or suggestions regarding our Privacy Policy, please contact us by email at\u00a0<\/span>help@myfamilyaid.com<\/span><\/a><\/p>\n
